Astrophotography Competition

The month of November brings 🥶chills, 🇬🇧tea parties and most importantly, clear 🌌skies! So SEDS Celestia, the Astronomy, Astrophysics and Space Tech club of BITS Goa brings an Astrophotography Competition🔭📷🏆 for y'all!

Rules

  • You can capture images of any part of the sky, even the moon!
  • You can submit up to 2 images
  • While uploading, you need to rename the file as (ID-Image number). So the first image will be ID-1.XXX. Please follow the format, we don't want to disqualify amazing art just because of some rule.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited. Respect others' and your work :\)
  • The winners will get rewarded with cash prizes and will get featured on our instagram.
